Information Collection

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All game progress and scores are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to any external server.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All data generated by this app (game progress, scores, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device. This data is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time through the app's Settings menu by selecting "Reset All Data." You may also uninstall the app to remove all associated data from your device.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any personal information from any user, including children. The app is designed as an educational tool for learning the Korean alphabet (Hangul) and does not require any personal information to function.
